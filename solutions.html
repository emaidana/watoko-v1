01 Cooperatives & Farmers

For the hardest ground on Earth.

60% of the world's arable land is farmed by smallholders. They carry the knowledge. Until now, they did not carry the intelligence layer. We put the same operational infrastructure large-scale agriculture has always had into the hands of the farmers that feed most of the world. Every season, their data contributes to a biological record that grows more valuable over time.

  • Field intelligence in 2,000+ languages — delivered through WhatsApp, SMS, and voice where farmers already communicate
  • Autonomous crop decisions: when to irrigate, when to harvest, what inputs to apply — run by agents that learn from every season
  • Direct market access with verified lot passports and transparent pricing — no middlemen, no information asymmetry
  • Instant mobile money payments the moment a trade confirms
  • Parametric insurance triggered automatically by satellite data — no claims process, no delays

Brain + Ecosystem

The intelligence, market access, and financial services that large-scale agriculture has always had — deployed for the first time on the hardest ground on Earth. Every field decision feeds a biological record that makes the next season sharper.

  • Complete supply chain visibility from field to port — every lot traceable, every handoff documented
  • Automated EUDR compliance and export documentation generated in seconds
  • Trade infrastructure with AI-verified quality scores and origin data
  • Integrated logistics, credit, and insurance through a unified agent mesh

Full Platform

Brain for field intelligence. Market for trade. Ecosystem for operations. Labs for science. 1 platform orchestrating every function. Soil microbiome, plant genomics, climate exposure — biological data flows through the same mesh as your trades and logistics. That data is an asset. We treat it like one.

03 Buyers & Exporters

For trust that survives the port.

Not a story about visiting the farm. A biological record of what the crop contains — not just where it was grown. Every lot carries biological provenance: soil composition, microbial context, climate exposure, varietal genetics. Verifiable at every handoff. Defensible under every audit. Biology is the authenticity layer no narrative can fake.

  • Verified supply chains with complete data passports for every lot — origin, processing, farmer payment, all documented
  • AI-predicted quality scores generated 30 days before harvest
  • EUDR due diligence statements generated in seconds — not weeks of manual documentation
  • Forward contracts backed by real yield predictions from satellite and field data
  • Same-day farmer payment verification attached to every shipment

Market + Compliance

Every lot carries a verifiable data passport — soil microbiome, climate exposure, varietal genetics, processing record, settlement proof. The passport carries biological provenance: what the crop contains, how the biology of that place shaped it, and what that means for the buyer on the other end.
