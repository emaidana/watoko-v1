Watoko Grant Program

Intelligence for every farmer. Subsidized hardware, free onboarding, and pay-as-you-grow pricing — because the science of life, the data of complex environments, and the agents that read them should not depend on what a farmer can afford today.

1
Apply

Fill out a simple application with your farm details, location, and crops.

2
Review

Our team reviews your application within 2 weeks and confirms eligibility.

3
Deploy

Watoko installs sensors, activates satellite coverage, and configures your agents.

4
Grow

Start receiving intelligence immediately. Pay only as your yields and income grow.

What's Covered

Everything you need to get started

Sensor Hardware

IoT soil sensors (moisture, pH, NPK, temperature) installed at optimal points across your plots. LoRaWAN gateway included.

Onboarding & Setup

Complete site assessment, satellite activation, agent configuration, and farmer training — all handled by the Watoko team.

First-Season Insurance

Parametric crop insurance for your first growing season. Automatic payouts via mobile money if drought, flood, or pest events occur.

Training & Support

Hands-on training for farmers and cooperative managers. Ongoing WhatsApp support and periodic field visits during the first year.

Eligibility

Who qualifies for a grant

  • Smallholder farmers with less than 5 hectares of cultivated land
  • Cooperatives and farmer organizations across the African continent — with priority cohorts in East and West Africa, and a growing presence wherever complex environments demand new intelligence
  • Farms growing priority crops: coffee, tea, cocoa, maize, rice, sorghum, millet, cassava, banana, avocado, macadamia, sesame, horticulture, livestock
  • Willingness to contribute anonymized soil, microbiome, and crop data to Watoko Labs — the same record that informs research on plant genomics, biomolecular discovery, and closed-loop life-support
  • Active mobile money account for receiving payments and insurance payouts
