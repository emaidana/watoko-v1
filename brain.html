How It Works

From sensor to decision in minutes

Brain operates in a continuous loop — sensing, observing, analyzing, deciding, and delivering. Every cycle makes the next one smarter. Every decision weights the biological health of the soil alongside yield and economics.

1
Sense
IoT sensors collect soil data every 15 minutes — moisture, pH, NPK, temperature
2
Observe
Satellites capture daily imagery; drones provide on-demand high-res scans; cameras monitor pests
3
Analyze
AI agents fuse multi-source data, run micro-simulations for each plot
4
Decide
Agents determine the lowest-input path and classify by autonomy level — act or recommend
5
Deliver
Autonomous actions execute immediately; recommendations sent via WhatsApp with one-tap confirm

Works Everywhere

Offline-first by design

We built for complex environments — places where networks fail, infrastructure is thin, and the signal drops. Rural Africa was the training ground. The same architecture runs in sealed greenhouses, remote research stations, and closed-loop habitats. Intelligence does not stop when the signal does.

Edge AI on LoRaWAN

Sensors transmit over LoRaWAN with 15km range — no cellular network required. On-device models process data at the edge.

SMS & USSD Fallback

Every recommendation can be delivered via SMS or USSD on basic phones. Voice Agent works over standard phone calls — no data needed.

Store-and-Forward

Actions queue locally and sync when connectivity returns. No data loss, no missed decisions, no gaps in the timeline.

Offline Maps & Models

Satellite imagery and crop models cached locally. Field teams access full farm intelligence without a connection.

Mesh Networking

Nearby devices relay data through mesh networks, extending coverage across valleys, hills, and areas without towers.

Solar-Powered Hardware

All field sensors run on solar. No grid power, no batteries to replace. Deploy and forget — they just work.

Cross-Product Connections

Brain feeds the entire platform

Every observation Brain makes flows into Market, Ecosystem, and Labs — and their data flows back to make Brain smarter.

Brain ↔ Market

Yield predictions power forward contracts. Harvest quality data sets fair pricing. Market price signals optimize harvest timing and next-season crop selection.

Brain ↔ Ecosystem

Farm performance data enables credit scoring. Satellite data triggers insurance payouts. Credit enables farmers to invest in better inputs.

Brain ↔ Labs

Every sensor reading, soil microbiome sample, and satellite image feeds research models — from smallholder farms on Earth to closed-loop life-support trials in orbit. Labs returns optimized protocols for plant genomics, climate-resilient breeding, and biomolecular discovery that improve Brain's recommendations every season.
