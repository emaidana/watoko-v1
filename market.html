Sustainability & Carbon Agent

Carbon footprint per lot, sustainability premiums, environmental scorecards

Autonomous

Frequently asked questions about Market

The Price Intelligence Agent aggregates real-time data from African spot markets, international commodity exchanges, currency rates, futures, and local supply/demand signals. It produces a fair price range per commodity per location, updated continuously, and shows its work — every farmer, cooperative, and buyer can see exactly what inputs the number depends on. Both sides of every trade see the same transparent pricing.

All trades use escrow — buyer funds are held until delivery is confirmed via GPS tracking and quality verification at destination. If there's a dispute, the Dispute Resolution Agent mediates using contract terms, delivery receipts, and quality grades. Funds are released only when both parties agree or after automated resolution.

Market handles export documentation through the Compliance Agent. For individual farmers, we facilitate aggregation through cooperatives that hold export licenses. The EUDR Compliance Agent auto-generates the deforestation-free documentation required for EU markets.

The EUDR Compliance Agent automatically maps your farm boundaries via GPS, monitors forest cover change using satellite imagery, and generates due diligence statements. This process is continuous and automated — your compliance documentation is always current and audit-ready.

Market charges a small transaction fee on completed trades — typically 1-3% depending on volume and commodity type. There are no listing fees, no subscription fees for basic access, and no hidden charges. See our pricing page for full details.
